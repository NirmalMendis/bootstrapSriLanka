Wildlife

Sri Lanka's wildlife sanctuaries are a class of protected areas providing shelter for many species of widlife. Administered by the Department of Wildlife Conservation, this sanctuaries are open to visit. There are many popular sanctuaries in Sri Lanka with places for accomodation as well. Few most popular sanctuaries are :

  • Bar Reef
  • Giant's Tank
  • Kokkilai
  • Peak Wilderness
  • Udawatta Kele
